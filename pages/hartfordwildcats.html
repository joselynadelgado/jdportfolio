Hartford Wildcats

This project allowed me to create visually compelling and meaningful logos that represent the spirit and identity of this dynamic youth sports organization. Through a careful balance of colors, typography, and symbols, I aimed to create logos that are not only visually striking but also effectively communicate the program's identity. Whether it's the fierce representation of a wildcat or the vibrant combination of team colors, these logos serve as powerful visual representations of the Hartford Wildcats Youth Football and Cheer program.
