I was born in / Soy de Caguas

Through intimate conversations and personal anecdotes, I was born in / Soy de Caguas provides a profound understanding of the complexities of migration, cultural adaptation, and the resilience of the human spirit. From cherished memories to the struggles she overcame, my grandmother's narrative serves as a tribute to the strength and determination of individuals who embark on new chapters in unfamiliar lands.
