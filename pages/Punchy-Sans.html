Punchy Sans

Ecography is a method of engaging with language in and of our environment. I explored what it means to shorten the distance between our screens and our local environment by observing, gathering, exploring, and learning basic type design as a framework to think about where we are and how we work more critically. I found parallels of circles with hole punches, then developed letterforms to embody those ideas.
