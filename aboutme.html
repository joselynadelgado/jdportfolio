Hi! I'm Jojo.

I'm a graphic designer based in CT. I've received my BFA from the University of Connecticut. I also studied design internationally at Central Saint Martins in London.

I’m passionate about creating playful and interactive designs as a way to keep the work fun and engaging. I’ve been able to gain experience in many areas of design, including publication design, industrial design, coding, and motion graphics.
